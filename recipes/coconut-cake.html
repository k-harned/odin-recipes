Coconut Cake

Description:

How to spice-up a simple box cake into a show-stopping explosion of coconut and chocolate.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Devils Food Cake box
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 can Unsweetened Coconut Milk
  • 1/2 cup Sour Cream
  • 1/2 cup melted Butter

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350deg F
  2. Grease two 9in cake tins
  3. Beat together butter, eggs, sour cream, coconut milk and cake mix until fully combined (about 2 min)
  4. Pour mix into cake tins
  5. Bake at 350 for 25 minutes (until toothpick comes out clean)
