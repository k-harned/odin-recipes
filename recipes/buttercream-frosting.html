Homemade Coconut Buttercream Frosting

Description

Silky and creamy coconut buttercream frosting, the perfect topping for any sweet treat!

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 cups unsalted butter (softened)
  • 5 cups confectioners' sugar (sifted)
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 5-6 tbsp canned unsweetened coconut cream
  • (OPTIONAL) 1-2 tsp coconut extract

Steps:

  1. Using handheld or stand mixer, beat butter on medium-high until smooth and creamy (about 5 minutes)
  2. Add 2 cups confectioners' sugar, beat on med-low until moistened.
  3. Gradually add in remaining sugar one cup at a time
  4. Add salt and coconut milk
  5. Beat until light and fluffy
